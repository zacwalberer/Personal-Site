American Outlaws

The American Outlaws are an unofficial supporters' group for the United States men's national soccer team and United States women's national soccer team. As described by ESPN they are "a raucous group of U.S. supporters".

  • Project: Branding
  • Role: Indentity Design
  • Online: theamericanoutlaws.com

Design

The brand identity for the Phoenix chapter needed a major reconstruction. This chapter is a part of the National American Outlaws Association, who is the official fan organization of the US Men's National Soccer Team. The goal was to create a simple visual identity which was centered around a new logo, and would be implemented on a scarf (because soccer fans love their scarves).

The key elements to be implemented into the design were: the Arizona flag, American colors and iconic imagery, and a font that could be used along with the revamped identity.

For more information check out the American Outlaws.
