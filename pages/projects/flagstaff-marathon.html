Flagstaff Marathon

Annual marathon event and race held in Flagstaff, AZ.

  • Project: Identity Design
  • Role: Visual Designer

Background

As a former runner and resident of Flagstaff, the 11th edition of the Flagstaff Marathon was great to be a part of. The event will kick off in September of 2016 with hundreds of participants from all over Arizona and the nation.
