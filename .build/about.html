Hey, I'm Zac Walberer.

I’m a designer and developer happliy living in Tempe, AZ with my beautiful wife and non-existent dog. I am currently the Director of Design for Redemption Church, leading the design and communication teams throughout the ten congregations. My design has a focus on web, brand and typography; UX/UI and front-end development with little experience in Javascript. I have worked on both brand and product design with agencies like Design Forest, Brand Cure and MMS, as well as in-house with the Phoenix Suns and Redemption Church.
